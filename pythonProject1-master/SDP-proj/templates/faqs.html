Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1

What types of vehicles do you offer for rent?

Answer

We offer a wide range of vehicles including cars,bikes and buses. You can choose the one that suits your needs and preferences.

Question 2

What are the rental requirements?

Answer

To rent a vehicle, you need to be at least 18 years old and have a valid driver's license. You will also be required to provide a valid ID and a credit card for the rental deposit.

Question 3

How can I make a reservation?

Answer

You can make a reservation online through our website or by calling our customer service. Simply select the desired dates, location, and vehicle type, and follow the instructions to complete the reservation.
